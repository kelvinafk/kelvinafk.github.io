Which country produces the most bestseller books?

United Kingdom is the winner! This may be due to the widespread coverage of English in the world, meaning that more people purchase English books than books in other languages.

Who has written more than one bestseller book?

J.K. Rowling has more than one bestseller books in the chart. She has written the Harry Potter fantasy series, which has gained immense popularity and recognition among the globe.

Here is a simple way to start your reading!

Find a comfortable place, such as a library or a cafe, make sure there is adequate light.
Begin with the book cover, content page and preface to make yourself more familiar with the content.
Take notes whiile you are reading, which can make you more concentrated.
